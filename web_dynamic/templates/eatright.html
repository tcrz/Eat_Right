About

Eat Right is an app that will help you count your daily calorie intake. by providing you with an abundant food data and calories. The app is not only a calorie counter, it also has a section where you can share your favorite recipe with anyone who vists the app.

Our Mission

Eat Right is an app that will help you count your daily calorie intake. by providing you with an abundant food data and calories. The app is not only a calorie counter, it also has a section where you can share your favorite recipe with anyone who vists the app. Our mission is to help you stay healthy and for you to join a site where you can discover exotic meals from all over the world with just a click of a button. As long as you want to maintain a healthy lifestyle, you have come to the right place. because We want to join you on your journey foor a better life.

Our Story

The idea for the app came when we were brainstorming individually on what to create, we realised that we all had a similar idea. When we discussed it, It sounded so fun, doable, and helpful. It has not been an easy journey, the beginning is always the hardest part. we powered it through a difficult period and pushed on. With time, it started to come together the way we imagined it. Ideas we did not even think of, in the beginning, became part of it and it worked. We can only hope that you enjoy it as much as we enjoyed making this.
