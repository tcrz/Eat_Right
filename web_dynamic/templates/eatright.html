About us

Hello, and welcome to Eat Right, an app to help you eat right, everyday.

Our

Mission

Eat Right is an app that will help you count your daily calorie intake. by providing you with an abundant food data and calories. The app is not only a calorie counter, it also has a section where you can share your favorite recipe with anyone who vists the app.

Our mission is to help you stay healthy and for you to join a site where you can discover exotic meals from all over the world with just a click of a button. As long as you want to maintain a healthy lifestyle, you have come to the right place. because We want to join you on your journey foor a better life.

Our

Story

This app is created by 3 individuals with passion for creativity, who want a healthier life. We wanted to share our success for healthier life with everyone. The idea for the app came when we were brainstorming individually, but it was on the list of two people. when we decided to pitch-in the ideas to eachother. It did not even make it to the top 3 ideas. But then we discussed what it would have been like to create it. It sounded so fun, doable, and helpful. One thing led to another and it winded up becoming our 3 week project.

It has not been an easy journey. Infact, when we started it, we even considered if we made a mistake taking it in as a project. But the Beginning is always the hardest part. we powered it through a difficult time and we started it. It was not easy but it started to come together the way we imagined it. Ideas we did not even think of, in the beginning, became part of it and it worked. We can only hope that you enjoy it and it helps you reach your better and healthier self.
